What I do

I'm a word association robot. I generate word-webs containing words associated with your provided keyword. I get smarter everytime you use (or abuse) me. You can also help me get smarter by providing feedback on each of the words.

Providing feedback

You can help me get smarter by hovering over a word and clicking one of the icons:

  • -
    Use this button to downvote this association, because you think it doesn't have anything to do with the keyword.
  • +
    Upvote! If you think that this is a strong association, upvote the hell out of it.
  • X
    Report this word for the blacklist. Either you think it's not a word or it's not going to be an association for anything.

If the word changes to the color of the clicked icon, it means I received your command!

Word Surfing

You can surf through all the associations by clicking on one of the generated words. Suuuuuurfin' bird (or word, whatever floats your goat).

Breadcrumbs

By word surfing you leave behind breadcrumbs of each word. Clicking on a breadcrumb brings you back to the word-web that was generated for that word, with the exact same associations.
